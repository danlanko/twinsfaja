About Us

Twinsfaja Nigeria Limited was incorporated under the companies and Allied Matters Act 1990 in september, 2007 RC. 70706 and commenced operations immediately with branches across Lagos State

The Company is basically operating as a Trading Company and also Manufacturer Representatives in the area of Building Materials. We specialize in the sale of Quality Building Materials

Twins Faja Nigeria Limited also proffer professional services in Construction, Building and Civil Engineering, and has recorded tremendous success in executing projects both in public and private sector of the Nigeria economy.

Twins Faja Nigeria Limited has grown and expanded since its formation in 2007 due to the experience and professionalism of our management and technical staff. Our strength is based on our ability to bring together active professionals that has acquired enormous exposure in sales and marketing.

Our Mission

At Twins Faja Nigeria Limited our mission is to continue being a leader in the building industry and continue to meet out customers needs by providing excellent customer service

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the biggest supplier and manufacturer of building materials. Hence, we always use pragmatic approach in all our businesses. We go extra length to satisfy all our customers while following reasonable business ethics

Our Core Values
